How It Works

Plan, price, order, delivered.

1. Open the trade planner.

Start in the trade workspace, or download our DXF blocks and drop them straight into your own drawings.

2. Design the kitchen.

Lay out the cabinets, choose your color, and watch the price update as you go. 80+ blocks, 15 colors.

3. Send us the order.

Submit the order from the planner. We confirm, quote, and build. 12 to 14 weeks from order to delivery.

4. Delivered to site.

Fully assembled, individually wrapped, to site or your loading dock. Ready to install.

Materials + Construction

All About The Cabinets.

Every block in the catalog is the same construction, so one spec covers the whole project.

Painted beech exterior.

Solid beech frames, spray finished in a hardwearing eggshell. Smooth, durable, and built to handle daily kitchen use.

Oak veneer interior.

Oak veneer lined interiors throughout. The inside is as considered as the outside.

Dovetailed drawers.

Traditional dovetail joints on every drawer. Built to last and built to look it.

Brass butt hinges.

Simple, solid brass hardware. A hinge that does one job and does it for decades.

Lamello installation.

Face frames clip on with Lamello clips for a clean, precise finish. Fast, straightforward installation on site.

Made in England.

Built to order in a North Norfolk workshop. Nothing is built on spec and nothing goes to waste.
