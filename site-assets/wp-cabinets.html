The Cabinets.

Every block in the system. Four cabinet types, 15 paint colors, one level of quality. Just what you need for a kitchen, nothing you don't.

The System

Four types. One catalog.

Every PL/NTH kitchen is built from the same catalog of blocks. Pick the blocks that fit your walls, and they arrive as one kitchen.

Floor Cabinets

30 blocks

Doors, drawers, corners, bins and open shelves. 35" tall, in 20" to 42" widths.

Wall Cabinets

13 blocks

Single, double and glazed uppers. 30" tall, 14" deep.

Counter Cabinets

9 blocks

Counter-to-ceiling dressers, plain or glazed. 50" tall, 14" deep.

Tall Cabinets

9 blocks

Full-height larders and pantry storage, with doors and drawers.

The Catalog

We don't print a brochure. Every block lives in the planner with its real size and its real price, so the catalog you browse is the catalog you order from. Drop blocks onto your walls and watch the total move as you go.

The Materials

Built properly.

Painted beech frames

Exterior face frames in solid beech, painted in the workshop, not on site.

Oak lined interiors

Every cabinet is lined in oak veneer. Open the door and it looks as good as the front.

Dovetailed drawers

Dovetail joints on every drawer, with soft-close as standard.

Brass butt hinges

Proper brass hinges, not concealed plastic clips.

Arrives assembled

Every cabinet ships fully built. Blocks connect with Lamello clips for a fast, precise install.

Made to order

Built in a North Norfolk workshop in the hangars of a former RAF airfield. Nothing is made on spec, nothing goes to waste.
